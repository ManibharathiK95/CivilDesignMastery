Load Calculations

Loads are forces acting on a structure. Accurate calculation is critical for safe design.

Types of Loads

  • Dead Load (DL): Permanent loads from structure weight
  • Live Load (LL): Movable loads like people, furniture
  • Wind Load (WL): Lateral pressure from wind
  • Seismic Load (SL): Earthquake-induced forces
