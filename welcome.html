Welcome!

Welcome to Civil Design Mastery! This course is designed to take you from a beginner to an expert civil designer. It consists of three comprehensive phases:

Phase 1: Entry-Level Civil Design Concepts

Learn the fundamental concepts of civil design, including structural elements, loads, safety codes, and basic exercises with quizzes. Each lesson is designed to be engaging and clear.

Phase 2: Concrete Design

In-depth coverage of concrete design for various structural elements. Learn rules, formulas, sample layouts, and detailed design of beams, slabs, columns, and foundations.

Phase 3: PT (Post-Tensioned) Design

Advanced PT design concepts with detailed element design, sample calculations, and practical exercises. Prepare for real-world PT design scenarios confidently.
