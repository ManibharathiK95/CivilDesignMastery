Basic Structural Concepts

This lesson covers the fundamental concepts of structural engineering, essential for designing safe and stable structures.

Stress & Strain

Stress is the internal resistance per unit area; strain is the deformation per unit length.

Beam, Column, Slab

  • Beam: Resists bending from loads
  • Column: Transfers axial loads to foundations
  • Slab: Horizontal element distributing loads
